PERSON OF THE WEEK

Asim Hussain (@jawache) is the EMEA Lead for Regional Cloud Advocacy at Microsoft where he helps
developers fall in love with the Azure Cloud Platform. He has almost 20 years experience working for
organizations such as the European Space Agency and Google and as a freelancer and startup founder.
Asim created a number of courses on JavaScript-related technologies and published the books
Introduction to Machine Learning with TensorFlow.JS and Angular From Theory To Practice. He speaks regularly
at conferences around the world about machine learning, JavaScript, sustainable development, and Dev
Rel. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Asim!
